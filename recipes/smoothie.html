Strawberry Smoothie


Description

This strawberry smoothie recipe blends strawberries with milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla for an icy-cold treat.

Ingredients

  • 8 strawberries, hulled
  • 3½ cup skim milk
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 cubes ice, crushed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a blender.
  3. Add ice and blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
